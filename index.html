About me

I am a theoretical physicist working on string theory,focusing on its aspects as quantum gravity-- in particular, the holographic principle and quantum black holes.

I received Ph.D. in physics from the University of Tokyo in Japan in 2019. Currently I am an assistant professor at the University of Osaka in Japan . See also my Curriculum Vitae.
